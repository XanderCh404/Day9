How to help?
You too can help ARS, just:
Drop by our shelters, thrift stores or provincial office and make your donation in-person
Make a gift of securities (stocks, bonds and mutual funds)
Mail your gift to: Nova Scotia SPCA PO Box 38073 STN Burnside Dartmouth, NS B3B 1X2
Cheques can be made out to Nova Scotia SPCA
Plan and host a fundraiser or event
Pick up the top-needed supplies on our shelter wish list
Give us a call at 1-844-835-4798 or e-mail info@spcans.ca for assistance with making your gift.
We would love to help you!
