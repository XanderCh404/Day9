There are many different designs from across the world, here are a few examples:

The Zweihander is a german greatsword, used during the 1500s. The sword was sometimes made up to 7ft long, or 2.1m making it difficult to use while on foot
The Tanto is a japanese knife, invented during the heian period, (794-1185) and is still used today. the tanto's blade could be anywhere from half a ft to one ft long, or 15 to 30 cm
The Kijij is a turkish curved sword, invented around 80BC, becoming popular around the 1500s and continued use into world war 2 the Kijij's blade would commonly be around 2 to 3 ft long, or from half a meter to a meter long
